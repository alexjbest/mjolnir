Lemma7.0.10Krasner's lemma
Let \(f(x) \in \cO_K[x]\) be irreducible and monic, with roots \(\alpha_1,\ldots,\alpha_d\) in \(K\). Suppose \(\beta \in \overline{K}\) with \(|\beta-\alpha_1| \lt |\beta - \alpha_i|\) for all \(i = 2,\ldots,d\). Then \(\alpha_1 \in K(\beta)\).
Proof
Let \(L = K(\beta)\), \(L' = L(\alpha_1,\ldots,\alpha_d)\). Then \(L'/L\) is Galois and for \(\sigma\in \Gal(L'/L)\) we have \(|\beta-\sigma(\alpha_1)| = |\sigma(\beta-\alpha_1)| = |\beta-\alpha_1|\) implying that \(\sigma(\alpha_1) = \alpha_1\) and so \(\alpha_1 \in K(\beta)\).
