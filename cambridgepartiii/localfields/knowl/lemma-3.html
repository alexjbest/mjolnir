Lemma2.0.14
Let \(\ab_1\) and \(\ab_2\) be non-trivial absolute values on a field \(K\) then TFAE:
  1. \(\ab_1\) and \(\ab_2\) define the same topology on \(K\).
  2. \(|x|_1\lt 1 \iff |x|_2 \lt 1\).
  3. \(|x|_2 = |x|_1^c\) for some \(c \gt 0\).
If these conditions hold we say that \(\ab_1\) and \(\ab_2\) are equivalent.
Proof
1) \(\implies\) 2) \(|x|_1 \lt 1 \iff x^n \to 0\) as \(n \to \infty\) w.r.t. \(\ab_1 \iff x^n \to 0\) w.r.t. \(\ab_2\) iff \(|x|_2 \lt 1\).
2) \(\implies\) 3) Pick \(a \in K^*\) with \(|a|_1 \lt 1\). Let \(x\in K^*\)\(m,n\in \ZZ\)\(n \gt 0\).
