Example3.2.3
\(K = k((t)) = \) the field of Laurent power series \[ = \left\{ \sum_{n \ge n_0} a_n t^n : a_n \in k\right\}.\] We have \(v ( \sum a_n t^n ) = \min \{n : a_n \ne 0\}\). Then \(\cO = k[[t]] =\) ring of power series in \(t\). We get \(m = \{ f \in k[[t]]: f(0) = 0\}\) and we have \(\cO/m = k\).
