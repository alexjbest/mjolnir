Proof
\(\QQ\cap \ZZ_p = \{x\in\QQ: |x|_p \le 1\}= \{\frac{a}{b} \in \QQ : p \nmid b,a,b\in\ZZ\} = \ZZ_{(p)}\). Let \(\frac{a}{b} \in \ZZ_{(p)}\) i.e. \(a,b \in \ZZ\), \(p\nmid b\). For each \(n \ge 1\) we can pick \(y_n \in \ZZ\) s.t. \(by_n \equiv 1 \pmod{p^n}\) implying \(by_n \to_p 1\) as \(n \to \infty\). This implies that \(ay_n \to \frac{a}{b}\) as \(n\to \infty\). Hence \(\ZZ\) is dense in \(\ZZ_{(p)}\). Now for\(\QQ\) is dense in \(\QQ_p\) and \(\ZZ_p \subset \QQ_p\) being open give that \(\QQ\cap \ZZ_p\) is dense in \(\ZZ_p\).
