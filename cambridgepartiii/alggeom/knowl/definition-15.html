Definition1.2.28Sheafification
Given a preheaf \(\F\) on \(X\) there exists an associated sheaf \(\F^+\) and a morphism \(\Theta \colon \F \to \F^+\) with the universal property that for any sheaf \(\G\) on \(X\) and morphism \(\phi\colon \F \to \G\) there is a unique morphism \(\psi\colon \F^+ \to \G\) such that \(\phi = \psi \Theta\). We construct \(\F^+\) as follows: For \(U\) open in \(X\) we let \[\F^+(U) = \left\{ \text{functions } s\colon U \to \coprod \F_p : \forall p \in U s(p) \in \F_p,\, \forall p \in U \exists \text{ open nbhd } W,\, t\in \F(W) \text{ with } s(q) = t_q \forall q\in Q\right\}\]
