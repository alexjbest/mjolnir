Example1.2.23
\((V,\cO_V)\) for \(V\) an irreducible affine variety \(V\) is a ringed space via its structure sheaf, it is locally ringed over the base field \(k\). If \(\Phi\colon V \to W\) is a morphism of affine varieties then there exists a morphism of lcoally ringed spaces over \(k\)\((\phi, \phi^\#)\colon (V, \cO_V) \to (W, \cO_W)\) given by \(\phi^\#(g) = g\circ \phi \in \cO_V(\phi^{-1}U)\) for \(g\in\cO_W(U)\).
