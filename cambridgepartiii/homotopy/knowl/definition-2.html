Definition1.3.1Relative homotopy groups
The relative homotopy group\(\pi_n(X,A,x_0)\) is the set of homotopy classes of maps \(f\colon I^n\to X\) such that
  1. \(f(\dd I^n)\subset A\).
  2. \(f(\sqcap^{n-1}) = \{x_0\}\).
For \(n\ge 2\) the usual formula defines a composition law on \(\pi_n(X,A,x_0)\). For \(n\ge 3\) the usual argument shows \(\pi_n(X,A,x_0)\) is an abelian group.
