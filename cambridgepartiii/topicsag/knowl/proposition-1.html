Proposition1.2.10
  1. For any \(p\in \Spec A\) \(\sO_p = A_p\).
  2. For any \(f\in A\) let \(D(f) = \{p\in \Spec A : f\notin p\} = \Spec A \smallsetminus V(f)\).
  3. \(\Gamma(\Spec A,\sO) = A\).
