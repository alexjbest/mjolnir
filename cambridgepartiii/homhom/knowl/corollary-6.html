Corollary1.8.10
Assume \(\cA\) has enough injectives and write \(\inj \cA \subset \cA\) for the full subcategory of injective objects. Then \[ K^+(\inj \cA) \cong D^+(\cA). \]
Proof
We have fully faithfulness by 1.8.8. To see that it is essentially surjective write down injective resolutions for complexes (see later).
