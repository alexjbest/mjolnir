Lemma1.7.3
Projective resolutions exist in \(\cA\) if \(\cA\) has enough projectives.
Proof
Let \(A\in \cA\), then there exists \[ 0 \to K_0 \to P_0 \to A \to 0 \] and inductively \[ 0 \to K_{n+1} \to P_{n+1} \to K_n \to 0 \] with \(P_i\) projective. We can splice these together to get \[ \cdots \to P_2 \to P_1 \to P_0 \to A \to 0. \]
