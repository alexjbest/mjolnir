Definition1.4.13Good pair
\((X,A)\) is a good pair if
  1. \(\exists U \subset X\) open and \(A \subset U\)
  2. \(\exists \pi \colon U \to A\) with \(\pi|_A = \id_A\).
  3. \(\pi \sim 1_{(U,A)}\) as maps of pairs from \((U,A)\) to itself.
(i.e. \(A\) is a deformation retract of \(U\)).
