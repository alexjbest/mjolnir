  1. \(C_0 = C_1 = \ZZ\), \(C_i = 0\) otherwise, \[ 0\to \ZZ \xrightarrow{\cdot 3} \ZZ \to 0. \] Then \(H_1 = 0\), \(H_0 = \ZZ/3\).
