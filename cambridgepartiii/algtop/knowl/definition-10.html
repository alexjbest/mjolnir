Definition1.3.10Singular chain complex
If \(X\) is a space, the singular chain complex of \(X\), \(C_*(X)\) is defined by \[C_n(x) = \langle e_\sigma : \sigma \colon \Delta^n \to X \text{ any continuous map}\rangle.\] Where \[d(e_\sigma) = \sum_{j=0}^{n} (-1)^j e_{\sigma\circ F_j} \in C_{n-1}(X)\] where \(F_j\colon \Delta^{n-1} \to \Delta^n\) is given by \(F_j(x_0,\ldots,x_{n_1} = (x_0,\ldots,0,\ldots,x_{n-1})\) with the \(0\) in the \(j\)th place.
