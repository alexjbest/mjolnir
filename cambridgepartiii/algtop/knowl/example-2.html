Example1.2.9

\(X\) is contractible if and only if \(X \sim \{p\}\).

Proof
\(X\) contractible \(\implies 1_X \sim c\), a constant map. Choose \(f\colon X \to \{p\}\), \(f(x) = p\) and \(g\colon\{p\} \to X\), \(g(p) = c\). Then \(g\circ f = c \sim 1\) and \(f\circ g = 1_{\{p\}}\). Converse: exercise.
